Benefit of Being a Pharmacist
You help other people be healthy.

A pharmacist is a person who gives out medications to patients that are prescribed by their physicians. They also help the doctor be aware of all the facts they would need to know about the medication. Some facts would be about the amount of dosages, or the side effects that could happen. Pharmacists can identify the medication by their biological, chemical, and physical properties. Their goal is to increase positive health care results and improve a patients' quality of life with minimum health risks.
